About Me

Creative, Active, Careful

A data scientist in Computer Vision

Dr. Truong is currently a postdoctoral researcher at Sejong University (Korea). He obtained the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science (CS) from Sejong University (Korea) in 2021, the M.Sc. degree and the B.Sc. degree in CS from the University of Science, VNU-HCMUS (Vietnam), in 2012 and 2009, respectively.

He is also a lecturer at the VNU-HCMUS and play a role as an instructor in several courses, such as computer systems, data structures & algorithms, and pattern recognition. He is a data scientist specializing in computer vision & pattern recognition. His research interests include Biometrics, Computer Vision, Image Analysis, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
